King Penguin

The king penguin is the second largest species of penguin, smaller, but somewhat similar in appearance to the emperor penguin,thay make gigantic colonies when they come in to shore during the mating season.

  • Scientific Name: Aptenodytes patagonicus
  • Average Length: 70 to 100 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 15 to 20 years
  • Habitat: southern oceans and sub-Antarctic

King penguin is the second largest species of penguin. They reach about 100 centimeters in length, and are named Aptenodytes patagonicus for the southern oceans and sub-Antarctic in which they make their homes.
